Meet the Stylist

Hi, my name is Jenni. I am an independent hair stylist, specializing in Color and Vivids, in beautiful downtown Marquette. With over ten years of experience, I can give you a fresh new look that suits your individuality and style!

I knew that I wanted to be a hair stylist from a very young age, styling my trolls hair in my grandmother’s salon and yes, even giving them haircuts! My grandma was a huge inspiration to me. She taught me how to sew and make clothes when I was very young. After high school, I was accepted into Michigan State University’s Apparel and Textile Design program and earned my Bachelor’s degree in fashion design.

I had big dreams to work in the fashion industry, but still really wanted to be a hair stylist, so I enrolled in Paul Mitchell Hair Expressions Academy in Rockville Maryland. After graduating, I landed a job at a high-end salon in San Francisco. I packed my bags and drove to California where I was involved with fashion shows and photo shoots. My free time was filled with classes to immerse myself in learning everything I could about cutting, styling and coloring hair. “JenniStylez Hair Design”, my own private studio salon in Silicon Valley, opened in 2011!

In 2014, I decided to move back home to Michigan! I’m originally from Traverse City, but fell in love with Marquette when visiting one summer. My free time is filled with hiking, mountain biking, swimming and watching my favorite shows with my boyfriend and two fur babies. I also enjoy attending hair shows, such as the Redken Symposium in Las Vegas. I absolutely love what I do! Hair styling is art, so book an appointment today and let your hair be my canvas!
